ABOUT US

Who we are

We launched our operations in January 2019 as a profit-making enterprise with the aim of establishing and sustaining the production of high quality, hygienic and nutritious PEPPER for the benefit of our community. This dream has been realised by a capable and determined team after months of feasibility studies, planning and nurturing ideas.

Location

The company and farm are located in Agona Nsaba, a town an hour away from the capital. The area is well known for its fertile soil and favourable climate which supports variety of crops. Our special relationship with the community allows us to provide expert ideas to local farmers on new sustainable farming practices to improve their yield.

Business

Our business structure is based on providing economic empowerment and financial protection for local farmers in the community in which the business is set up. We employ local farmers who combine ideas with agricultural experts to cultivate the land using both traditional and modern sustainable farming practices.

ASA FANKOBA PEPPER

ORGANIC

Our Fankoba peppers are cultivated using certified chemically untreated organic seedlings. The plants are supplied with organic fertilizers in the open field and there are no pesticides nor chemical applications.

LOCAL

Our goal is to produce quality pepper locally by employing indigenous farmers and using local resources such as our organic fertilizer. We see this as a great way to promote economic independence in our communities.

MADE WITH LOVE

From the idea to the farmers on the field, our work is driven by passion. By giving our best, we combine traditional and modern farming practices to produce fresh and flavorful Fankoba PEPPER that you will love.
